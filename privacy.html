Sandtris — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Sandtris is a single-player puzzle game. We designed it to respect your privacy completely: the app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

What we collect

Nothing. Sandtris has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs. We never ask for your name, email, contacts, location, photos, or any identifier.

Data stored on your device

Your game progress, settings, and high scores are saved locally on your device using the operating system's standard preferences storage. This data never leaves your device and is removed when you delete the app.

Network access

Sandtris does not require an internet connection to play and does not send any data to us or to any external server.

Children's privacy

Because we collect no data at all, Sandtris is safe for players of all ages, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
