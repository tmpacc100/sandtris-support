How to play

Shape the glowing tetromino as it falls — the instant it lands it crumbles into thousands of sand grains that pile up with real physics. Grow a connected mass of one colour past the match threshold and it collapses in a burst.

Specials

RAINPours the board's dominant colour from the sky to help finish a clear.
CLONECopies the current piece into NEXT so you can pile the same colour.
SHAKEJolts the board so steep piles collapse and merge.
VACUUMSucks the top band of sand into a vortex — an emergency clear.

FAQ

How do I lose? Watch the hourglass meter on the left. If sand chokes the spawn corridor, the run ends.

Does it need internet? No. Sandtris plays fully offline and collects no data — see our Privacy Policy.

Is my progress saved? Yes, locally on your device.
